## 📄 Publications

### 1. Logistic Regression-Based Credit Scoring for Loan Default Reduction
> *A Quantitative Framework* | 2026

A complete credit scoring framework developed on 15,000+ loan accounts, achieving a **12% reduction in loan defaults**. Covers WoE/IV feature engineering, scorecard calibration, interest rate sensitivity analysis (13%–14.5% corridor), and NPL cohort segmentation.

📂 [`paper1_credit_risk.md`](./paper1_credit_risk.md)

**Key topics:** Logistic regression · Probability of default · Credit scorecard · Interest rate sensitivity · NPL management

### 2. Customer Churn Prediction in Mobile Telecommunications Using K-Means Clustering and Ensemble Predictive Modeling
> 2026

An end-to-end churn prediction and intervention framework applied to **15 million+ customer records**. Combines K-means behavioral segmentation with XGBoost prediction (AUC = 0.863). A/B tested SMS intervention achieved **24% relative churn reduction** across 1.2M high-value customers.

📂 [`paper2_churn_prediction.md`](./paper2_churn_prediction.md)

**Key topics:** K-means clustering · Gradient boosting · Churn prediction · A/B testing · Mobile money analytics · Telecom

---

### 3. A Quality Evaluation Framework for AI-Generated Mathematical Reasoning in STEM Training Datasets
> 2026

Proposes the **Mathematical Reasoning Quality (MRQ) Framework** — a five-dimensional rubric (Logical Validity, Solution Pathway Diversity, Notational Rigor, Error Transparency, Pedagogical Clarity) for evaluating AI-generated mathematical solutions. Applied across proof-by-induction, ODEs, linear algebra, and real analysis case studies.

📂 [`paper3_ai_math_evaluation.md`](./paper3_ai_math_evaluation.md)

**Key topics:** AI evaluation · Mathematical reasoning · STEM training data · LaTeX · Proof verification · Dataset quality
